rock_paper_scissors_ssing_cpp

This code features playing a simple game of rock, paper, scissors with a computer coded using C++. The code will ask for your name with only using 1 word and ask for your age. The computer will explain the rules for you and how you can play before inputing your choice. After inputing your choice, the computer will reveal the choice it chose. Finally, the computer will compare both choices from the player and the computer itself and will declare the winner or a draw.
